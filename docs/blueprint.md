# **App Name**: U.A.E.C.G.

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Dynamic display of featured content, including a Hero section, mission highlights, recent news/events, and gallery previews, providing a snapshot of U.A.E.C.G.'s activities and values.
- Informational Pages: Dedicated pages for 'About Us,' 'Activities,' 'News,' 'Publications,' and 'Gallery,' offering comprehensive information about the organization's history, initiatives, achievements, and resources.
- Contact and Membership: A contact page with a submission form for inquiries, along with clear contact information and a prominent call-to-action for membership enrollment.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Institutional Blue (#003f99) for main CTAs, titles, active links, progress bars, and key navigation elements, symbolizing authority and trust.
- Secondary color: Olive Green (#6B8E23, approximate) to complement the primary, used for section backgrounds, icons, button hovers, and secondary visual components, reflecting growth and harmony.
- Accent color: Gold/Bronze (#CD7F32, approximate) drawn from the logo's laurels, used for separators, animations, decorative underlines, and subtle accents on important elements, adding sophistication and prestige.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a serif font lending an elegant and high-end feel. Used to create a strong and classic style for the titles.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a modern sans-serif font will be used for body text for clear and easy to read text.
- Subtle fade-up, slide-in, and zoom-in effects using AOS (Animate On Scroll) for sections, cards, titles, and images as they come into view, with staggered delays to ensure a harmonious experience.
- Button backgrounds transition smoothly from blue to olive green on hover. Navigation links get a subtle underline. Cards and vignettes on rollover grow slightly or cast a shadow.
